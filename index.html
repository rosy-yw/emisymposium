About the symposium

About

Join us for the CUHK–UCL English-medium instruction symposium, held on 24 April 2026 at the Department of Curriculum and Instruction, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Under the theme English-medium instruction in the era of GenAI: Negotiating tradition and modernity, the symposium brings together leading voices from Hong Kong and the international research community to examine how EMI is being transformed by generative AI across higher education. The event addresses questions of human agency, participation, authorship, assessment, academic language, teacher development, research practice, and educational inequality in a rapidly changing multilingual landscape.
